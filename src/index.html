Who we are

We are an outpatient physical therapy office providing specialized care for people of all ages with varying injuries. ​ ​

Our Goal:​ ​ As an out-patient physical therapy office, we strive to deliver the highest quality of treatment by utilizing various techniques from evidence-based research to achieve optimal outcomes. Our staff are equipped with the skills and knowledge to assess various sport, orthopedic and neuromusculoskeletal conditions. We provide the highest standard of care and individual treatment plans.

What we do

What do we treat?

  • Acute and chronic sprains/strains
  • Bell’s palsy/Cerebral palsy
  • Bursitis
  • Fractures
  • Gait instability/balance
  • ITB syndrome
  • Myofascial pain syndrome/Chronic fatigue syndrome
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Stroke
  • Patellofemoral syndrome
  • Post-surgical procedures (Joint replacement, Spine disc replacement/decompression)
  • Frozen shoulder/impingement
  • Spinal conditions/Bulging disc
  • Tendinosis/Tendinitis
  • Thoracic outlet syndrome
  • TMJ dysfunction
  • Whiplash/car accident
